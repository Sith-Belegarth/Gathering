⚔  What We Are

What is Belegarth?

Belegarth Medieval Combat Society is a full-contact foam combat sport inspired by fantasy and medieval warfare. Participants build their own padded weapons and engage in organized battles, melees, and scenarios.

No experience is required. You need only the willingness to step onto the field.

Arrive curious. Leave battle-tested.
